Bio

Nokuthula is a strategic communications professional with a diverse cultural background. Born in Harare, raised in Kenya, South Africa and Zimbabwe, she thrives in engaging and communicating in different environments where she can feed off vibrant ideas and provide her own inspiring contributions. Having worked across areas of international development, development finance and corporate communications, creating innovative strategies, she enjoys performing assessments with a public relations focus and has strengthened her ability to think critically and navigate complex scenarios. She is passionate about storytelling and is often inspired by the impact individual experiences have on wider journeys of self discovery.

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxPretoria, where x = independently organized TED event.

At our TEDxPretoria event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.
