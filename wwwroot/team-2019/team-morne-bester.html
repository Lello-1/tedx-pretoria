Bio

I was born on 19 August 1996 and grew up in Pretoria. In high school I kept himself busy with sport and music at a national level. In university, I joined one of the biggest student organisations in the country where I was elected into a leadership position for two years. I am an aspiring industrial psychologist and plan on completing my honours degree in 2020. Academic performance is important to me, as well as being involved in other activities, showcased by my work at the SA SDG Hub, TuksFM News/Sports Department and Doxa Deo Worship Band.

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxPretoria, where x = independently organized TED event.

At our TEDxPretoria event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.
