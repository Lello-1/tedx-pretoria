Bio

Suzette’s journey evolved from marketing and media in the early 1990’s following tertiary education in communications and industrial psychology to business consulting and project management, to currently innovation for sustainability gigs.

These opportunities stretched over residing in several countries and continents including the Middle East and Europe.

Her purpose mantra goes like this: Travel as much as you can, as far as you can, as long as you can. Life's not meant to be lived in one place.

At our TEDxPretoria event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.
