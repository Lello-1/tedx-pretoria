Busisiwe Gumede

Twitter Handle
@Busisiwe_G
Talk Title
In a World Ruled by Youth, "Old and Experienced” is the New Obsolete

Bio

Busisiwe Gumede is a freelance broadcast journalist and columnist at Sowetan.

She regularly produces for several in-depth news programs, including eNCA’s current affairs show called CheckPoint.

Gumede is the author of a column called the Youth Chat in the Sowetan newspaper, touching on critical issues with a view of empowering young people to take action.

She is currently on the list of the Mail and Guardian’s top 200 Young South Africans 2015, showcasing the most influential young people using their careers to make a difference in their communities.

Busisiwe is one of eight youth representatives taking part in monthly Ulutsha dialogues, hosted by The Presidency, engaging regularly with the Deputy Minister Buti Manamela.

She also hosts regular youth related dialogues.

Prior to that, Busi served as a senior current affairs anchor at YFM and her community radio station, Alex FM.

These platforms granted Busisiwe the opportunity to interview notable political figures, including the ANC’s Gwede Mantashe, EFF leader Julius Malema, the DA’s Mmusi Maimane, Former AgangSA leader Maphela Ramphele just to mention a few.

Gumede also served as an ambassador of the South African Brewaries’ #YouDecide campaign that aims to create awareness about the dangers of underage drinking.

You can spot her at conferences and televised debates discussing pertinent issues affecting south Africans living in country celebrating more than 20 years of democracy.
