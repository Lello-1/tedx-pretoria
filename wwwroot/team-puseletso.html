Bio

Personal Development Coach, Business Woman, Business Mentor, Kidtrepreneurs Coach and a WEYAN.

Puseletso Modimogale is a wife and a mother of two Kidtrepreneurs, proud Mosotho who is very passionate about entrepreneurship, business mentoring, innovative ideas, networking and being the servant of God. Her purpose is to help others unleash their potential and identify their talents in order to use them to fulfil their purpose.

As a natural entrepreneur, Puseletso knew early on in her life that she will become a Somatologist when her grandmother had a stroke, she therapeutically comforted her by gently rubbing her hands which resulted with her limbs being functional. That then planted the seed and concept of wellness, that’s when Therapeutic Touch Studio was born where she is a Co-Founder. The company was registered in 2002.

She is an inspirational and phenomenal woman who dared to push the envelope in business arena and a force to be reckoned with. A true networker, outspoken, highly determined and skilful, yet compassionate when working with people.

She later developed passion in enterprise development, hence the establishment of a Non-Profit movement called W.E.Y Foundation (2010) that is aimed to INSPIRE, CONNECT and HELP Women realise their talents and unleash their potential in order to live a purposeful life. She is also working with The Hope Factory South Africa as The Enterprise and Supplier Development Programme Manager. She is one of the mentors of the TV show called Making Moves.

Puseletso won Regional Business achiever award 2007 through BWASA and featured in various TV shows and magazines such as Motswako, the Power within SABC 2, Shift, Motsweding, Truelove and Radio Metro Positive Talk. In 2010 she won the entrepreneurial TV show called Rise Mzansi, Innovation Award by The Hope Factory. 2015 Care-Giver Award Winner for Woman making the difference in their communities by MEC Mazibuko (Gauteng Department of Social development) to name the few.

Her passion: entrepreneurship and kidtrepreneurship
