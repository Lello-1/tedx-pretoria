Speakers

Kwandile Sikhosana

Twitter Handle
@Ukwandile
Talk Title
From A Shack A President

Bio

Founder of the Daveyton Book Club which has established 12 libraries and book clubs across the Country and working on establishing a University and the project is worth 6 billon and has passed its feasibility stage. I have represented South Africa at the AU Youth Parliament, British Parliament and the Youth To Youth Summit in Dubai.

I will be talking about how unapologetic I am to be a product of the Township and how that has made my story unique and beautiful . Often times people don't know or understand how much of a success it is for you to survive the social ills that surround you on a daily basis, how difficult it is to change the narrative of townships amongst the privilege and also empowering people from the same neighborhood when all they know is poverty and limited Opportunities . I will also be touching on my presidential aspirations and what legacy I will be leaving or inspiring people with through my role in education
