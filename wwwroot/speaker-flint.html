Speakers

Josh & Adelle, aka Flint, meet Spark

Twitter Handle
@FlintmeetSpark
Talk Title
Empowerment Through E-Books

Bio

Flint, meet Spark is the culmination of the lives of two closet musicians- Josh and Adelle. Started in mid-2012, the duo formed as if by accident, when they began playing their songs to one another. A friend heard them and jokingly requested to be their manager and find them a gig. He did, and Flint, meet Spark came to be.

2012 saw the duo honing their skills as a unit, while playing in local competitions in Joburg, and eventually winning one of these in early 2013. 2013 saw the duo reach new heights with opportunities to play shows at venues such as Openroom Productions in Johannesburg, as well as take a trip to Zambia alongside South African artist Jeremy Loops. While on that trip, fortune was kind and they heard that they had been listed to play at Oppikoppi in August that year.

Singer-songwriters at heart, their sound could best be described as folk, with an African influence. Their aim is to use melody and harmony to portray a message of hope.
