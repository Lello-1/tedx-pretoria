Bio

I got involved in the TedTalk was because I felt I needed some form of belonging I didn't really understand who I was as a person and I mean friends only go so far and on the internet all you got was people half naked, I mean yes of course it's cool to see half naked people but I guess sometimes you need something with substance, and then one day I stumbled on TedAnimations, now if there's one thing I like it's anything that's animated, cartoons are bae so the moment I got hooked on TedAnimations I delved deeper into TedTalks thus arousing my hunger to be involved now I though it was impossible until a friend of mine told me that the TedTalks do come to RSA then I knew I had to do the right thing and sign up.

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxPretoria, where x = independently organized TED event.

At our TEDxPretoria event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.
