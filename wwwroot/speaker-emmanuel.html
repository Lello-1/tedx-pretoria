Emmanuel Munano

Twitter Handle
@Manemmanuel
Talk Title
Rewriting the Pretoria’s narrative through Imagery

Bio

Emmanuel Munano was born inthe East of Pretoria in a town called Mamelodi. He Studied in the West (Hercules) and has been surviving in Pretoria Central (Sunnyside, Gezina).

As an aspiring social entrepreneur, Emmanuel is interested in forming platforms where photographers are able to showcase photography related to social documentary and street photography in South Africa. He would like to explore ways of telling people's stories and earning an income in the process.

He is a self-taught street based photographer based in the capital city trying to make sense of every day life in the capital city (Tshwane). As a visual story teller,  he is interested in translating research into imagery that will speak to the every day person on the streets. His approach is to master the dexterities of capturing the ‘deceive moment’ in an urban environment. A lot of my work is around the documenting the informal economy, growing population in capital cities and the profiling capital cities as cultural melting pots.
