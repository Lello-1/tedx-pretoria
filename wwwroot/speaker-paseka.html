Paseka Lesolang

Twitter Handle
@LesolangPaseka
Talk Title
My Journey to Save the Planet

Bio

Paseka Lesolang is an optimistic realist, inquisitive, enthusiastic fellow and an innovative visionary — Committed to progressive development and passionate about environmental sustainability. Paseka is a young entrepreneur of note. He has been part of several entrepreneurial ventures, where he learnt a great deal about entrepreneurship, commitment to a purpose, etiquette, focus, strategy, bootstrapping, humility, perseverance, and the value of time and money. He has participated in a Business Administration Learnership sponsored by the Forchini Group, and has done a course in Advanced Entrepreneurship at the University of Pretoria and is a BCom Management Degree candidate at the University of South Africa. He is an innovator and a multinational award winning entrepreneur for his green innovations. Among the several accolades, Paseka was one of 15 delegates to represent South Africa at the EXPO 2010 in Shanghai, China. His innovation was awarded the most Innovative Environmental Product at the Maker Faire Africa [Kenya]. He is the 2010 Entrepreneur of the Year — University of Pretoria. He was chosen as guest speaker and Programme Director of the 2010 Global Entrepreneurship Day at The Innovation Hub. Guest speaker at the African Leadership Academy's first Ideas Festival themed Entrepreneurial Leadership: Empowering the Youth. In 2012, he was 1 of 25 High Impact Entrepreneurs granted Fellowship of the Unreasonable Institute [USA]. He is the 1st Prize Winner of the 2012 JCI (Junior Chamber International) Social Entrepreneur Best Business Plan Competition and the recipient of the SOCAP (Social Capital Market) 2012 Scholarship [USA]. He is also the recipient of the Innovative Social Entrepreneurship award at the SAB Foundation 2012 Innovation Awards. He is the 2012 JCI Creative Young Entrepreneur Award recipient [Taiwan]. He is acknowledged as a One Young World (OYW) social innovator [UK] and in 2013 he was one of 3 innovators in Africa to represent the Resilient African Network in Silicon Valley [USA]. In 2014 and 2015 Paseka was the Green Youth Indaba guest speaker and panellist. He was also on of the 2014 Global Cleantech Innovation Programme award winners. Earlier this year (2015) he was the Standard Bank Water4Africa Competition Judge and received the Technology Top 100 Company (TT100) Da Vinci Award for Excellence in Management Systems. He is now the award recipient of the inaugural Green City Start-Up challenge and the recipient of the inaugural GreenOvation Start-Up Green Entrepreneur Award.
