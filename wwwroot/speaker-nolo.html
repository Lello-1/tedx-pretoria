Nolo Mokoena

Twitter Handle
@Nolo_Mokoena
Talk Title
The Scourge of The Peasantry

Bio

Entrepreneur and Young African Leader, Nolo Mokoena is a final year student majoring in Economics at the University of Pretoria, with a co-specialization in Agricultural Economics and Management. Previously chairperson of the Young Economists Society and Vice Chairperson of the faculty of his university, his key interests lie in innovation & strategy, mediation and dialogue, youth advocacy and agricultural reform. An aspirant polymath, he is a recent alumni of the South Africa-Washington International Program(SAWIP) in 2014, he spent his summer internship at Higher Achievement, an education focused NGO in the Washington D.C metropolitan area- focusing his efforts on the Washington Nationals' Youth Program. He is also a recent fellow of the ISFiT Dialogue Groups in Norway. Nolo is the founder of two university focused start-ups, Campus KNECT and TwentyFive, and is a co-founder of South Africa's first online house music publication, The Set Magazine. Aside from his entrepreneurial ventures and initiatives, he hopes to grow as a creative solutionist- making a considerable contribution providing alternative solutions in both private and public sectors of society. Nolo is both an opinion writer on News24's Voices platform and a speaker, and is a continual contributor to various organisations and publications through these mediums.
