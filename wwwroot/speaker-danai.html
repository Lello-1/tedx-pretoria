Danai Nhando

Twitter Handle
@Danai Nhando
Talk Title
Poverty, Literacy & the Pursuit of Justice

Bio

Danai Nhando is a Human Rights Lawyer turned Equitable Education Advocate. She is passionate about Africa, the education of its people and the upholding of justice based on principles of fairness and equity.

She is the Founder of Open Learning Tank- a social enterprise that trains educators and builds knowledge around the use and reuse of Open Educational Resources (OER) and Open Source eLearning technologies to democratize access to quality academic resources.

Danai believes that equitable learning advancement does not always require large amounts of resources and she offers professional advisory services to academic institutions on how to set up low-cost, high-impact eLearning initiatives.

She is a top author in the eLearning Industry, the largest online community of professionals involved in eLearning globally, and a contributing writer for the Mail & Guardian Thought Leader Blog.
